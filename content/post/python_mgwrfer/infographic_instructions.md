### Three Contextual Channels (Panel 1 reference)

- **Intrinsic** вҖ” unmeasured attributes of place directly shifting `y`. Statistical home: local intercept (MGWR) or individual fixed effect (MGWFER `Оұ_i`).
- **Behavioural** вҖ” place modulating the slopes `ОІ_k(u_i, v_i)`. Statistical home: spatially varying coefficient surfaces.
- **Indirect** вҖ” place shaping the *levels* of the covariates `x_k`. Statistical home: the bias term `Оҙ_k` in `ОІМӮ_k = ОІ_k + Оҙ_k`. This is the channel MGWFER exists to remove.
