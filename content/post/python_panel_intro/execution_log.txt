Key takeaways:
  1. POLS (0.0750) and Between (0.0662) use cross-sectional variation.
  2. FE = DVFE = 0.2103; FDFE = 0.2113. FD matches Within exactly only after absorbing year FE (TWFE).
  3. RE (0.1092) is a weighted average; CRE (0.2103) recovers the FE coefficient.
