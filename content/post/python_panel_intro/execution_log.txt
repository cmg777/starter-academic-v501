SECTION 11: Summary of All Methods
============================================================

Method         Coef         SE Description
----------------------------------------------------------------------
POLS         0.0750     0.0231  Pooled OLS — ignores panel structure
FDFE         0.2113     0.0792  First-differencing — eliminates individual FE
TDFE         0.2103     0.0812  Within estimator — time-demeaned FE
DVFE         0.2103     0.0812  Dummy variable — explicit individual dummies
RE           0.1092     0.0299  Random effects — GLS (assumes no correlation)
CRE          0.2103     0.0703  Correlated RE — Mundlak approach

Key takeaways:
  1. FD, TDFE, and DVFE produce identical coefficients (0.2103) — three
     ways to implement the same within estimator.
  2. POLS (0.0750) ignores unobserved heterogeneity and is likely biased.
  3. RE (0.1092) is a weighted average of between and within estimators.
  4. CRE (0.2103) bridges FE and RE — its within coefficient approximates FE.
