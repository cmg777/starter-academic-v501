### Key Equations on Screen

- **ATT** (Section 1, background): E[Y(1) − Y(0) | D=1] — the effect on the woredas that got a park, the estimand every estimator targets.
- **Static TWFE** (Eq. 1, Section 4, background): outcome on a park indicator plus district and region×year fixed effects — the baseline DiD.
