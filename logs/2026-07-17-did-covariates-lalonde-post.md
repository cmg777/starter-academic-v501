## The argument

On the LaLonde/Dehejia-Wahba non-experimental panel (185 NSW trainees + 15,992 CPS controls, pre 1975 /
post 1978), the ATT is estimated eight ways and judged against the experimental benchmark of **\$1,794**.
Covariates rescue the estimate **only when they enter the control group's counterfactual trend** â€” in the
level (additive) or the treatment effect they are inert.
